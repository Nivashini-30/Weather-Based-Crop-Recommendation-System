Soil Analysis

Our system analyzes key soil parameters including:

  • Nitrogen (N) levels - Essential for leaf growth
  • Phosphorus (P) content - Important for root development
  • Potassium (K) measurements - Crucial for overall plant health
  • Soil pH balance - Affects nutrient availability
  • Organic matter content - Improves soil structure

This comprehensive analysis ensures your crop recommendations are perfectly tailored to your soil's unique characteristics.
